### The Toku Region
The Ancient China/Japan inspired Toku Region is the setting of Pokemon Hearth. Being set in the distant past compared to mainline pokemon games, a lot of the terminology is borrowed from Pokemon Legends Arceus. For instance, there are no Pokemon Trainers in Toku. The term used instead is Pokemon Wielder.

### Learnsets
Hearth uses the Pokemon XY Learnsets as a fallback for all pokemon who don't have custom learnsets. The custom learnsets are not yet documented.
